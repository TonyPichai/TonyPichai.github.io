Hi! I’m Anthony. I’m an Artist and a Designer specialising in interactive technology for screen-based media and installation spaces.

I develop immersive mixed media experiences using creative coding, machine learning, interactive video, and custom built controllers.

Through my work, I like to use playful interactions to bridge the gap between the physical and digital world. often exploring themes about the human condition along the way. As I see it, life is as a continuous self referential play of creativity. Through the chaos and complexity, moments of relation, harmony and beauty appear. Exploring this is why I create.
