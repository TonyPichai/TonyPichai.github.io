Hi, I’m Anthony. I’m an Artist and a Designer specialising in Creative Development and Interactive Technology for screen-based media and installation spaces.

I develop a range of mixed media experiences such as, interactive AV games, custom built controllers, projection mapped reactive visuals and more!

Through novel interactions, I create spaces that explore the realities of psychological, perceptual and digital experiences, connecting us to questions about the life we share with technology and who we are today.
